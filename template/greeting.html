Why I need to use startuplink?

I have encountered that I often have opened pinned tabs in my browser. When I close browser and open it again my pinned tabs disappear.

To resolve this issue I wrote plugin for Chrome which allow to you to save your links and reopen it whenever you need.

You can find this plugin here

This service allows to you synchronize your tabs among your browsers.
