Why I need to use startuplink?

I noticed that I frequently keep important tabs pinned in my browser. However, whenever I close the browser and reopen it, the pinned tabs vanish.

To tackle this inconvenience, I developed a Chrome plugin that enables you to save your links and reopen them whenever required.

You can access this handy plugin here

This service also lets you synchronize your tabs across multiple browsers, making it even more convenient for you.
